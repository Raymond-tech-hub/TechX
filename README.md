# TechX

**From Curiosity to Legacy: Pioneering AI Excellence**

TechX is a dynamic young AI and technology company creating intelligent, accessible solutions that transform learning, automate workflows, and empower innovation worldwide.

Founded by 16-year-old Python & AI enthusiast **Diamond Ebenyo**, TechX started as a spark of curiosity and a dream to build meaningful AI. Today, it's a collaborative hub of four talented young creators pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence and technology.

## Our Flagship Projects

- **Lumina**  
  An AI-powered adaptive learning platform that makes education engaging and effective.  
  - Personalized lessons that adapt to each student's pace and style  
  - Gamified elements, rewards, and instant feedback to boost motivation  
  - Intelligent AI tutor and real-time guidance  
  - Teacher dashboard for performance insights, predictions, and data-driven decisions  
  Early experiments with students showed significant gains in engagement and academic performance.

- **Nova**  
  Our custom large language model (LLM) serving as a versatile, intelligent AI assistant for conversations, task automation, insights, and more.

- **Edulytics**  
  Analytics tool that brings education data and powerful insights together for better outcomes.
